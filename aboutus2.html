NAFULAMUKE PROJECT

Nafulamuke is a Giriama word that means lets wake up.
In my project I work with 10 girls and 7 boys aged 10-14 years where we always meet on Monday,Wednesday and Friday
and have a session with them on drug abuse. The main aim that made me to choose this age age group is that
I wanted them after getting the information to share it with their friends who are not in the project so that
our next generation will avoid using drugs as it is now. Alot of people use drugs because they lucked the information
on the effects of drug abuse hence by teaching them at this young age our next generation will avoid using drugs
